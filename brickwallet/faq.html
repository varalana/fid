Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open my Brick Wallet?
To open the Brick Wallet, simply use a hammer, pickaxe, or other method to crack open the protective masonry. We love seeing the ways our customers open their Brick Wallet! Show us with #BreakBrickWallet!
Can I get a replacement if my wallet breaks?
To encase your items again, show the official Brick Wallet certificate at any hardware store or construction site and they will gladly pour cement all over your cards and cash.
Can this be used as a weapon?
Brick Wallet LLC does not condone violence. Our wallets are tough! They can take all kinds of abuse without breaking!
