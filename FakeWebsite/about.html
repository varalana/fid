About Brick Wallet

Invented in 1999 when our founder, Denise Denser, accidentally dropped her wallet in a concrete mixer, The Brick Wallet™ has been protecting cash and cards for over a decade! Brick Wallet stores your valuables in solid concrete shaped like an adorable rectangular prism. If you try hard enough, it can even fit in your pocket!
